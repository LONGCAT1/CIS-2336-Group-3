This page will briefly tell you about the Galleria Mall.

Each year more than 30 million visitors visit the Galleria, which is also Texas' largest shopping center.


The mall spans 2.4 million square feet. The Galleria has 400 stores and resturants, 2 high-rise hotels, and 3 office towers.


There is also a full-size ice skating rink, which can be used for recreational and figure skating.


That's not all, there's also 2 swimming pools and a chilrdren's play area.

Addtionally, there are national and international banks, several salons, 2 tailor shops, and a post office.

Among the stores, there are high-fashion brands like Louis Vuitton, CELINE, and Gucci.

There's also Tiffany & Co, as well as H&M, American Eagle, and Urban Outfitters.
